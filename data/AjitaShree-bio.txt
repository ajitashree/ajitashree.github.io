I am PhD student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute Of Technology Kanpur, advised by Dr. Hamim Zafar. My research interest includes computational genomics, deep generative modelling, and probabilistic machine learning. I am currently working on developing unsupervised learning algorithms for the joint analysis of single-cell and spatial transcriptomics datasets (biorxiv). 

Before starting PhD, I worked as a Data Scientist at GE Technology Center - JFWTC and as a Research Engineer in the Material Science team at GE Global Research. I completed M.Tech in Computer Science and engineering from IIT Delhi, advised by Prof. Naveen Garg.
